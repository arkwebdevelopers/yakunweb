High Torque Connection

  • HT (High Torque) Connections are double shoulder designed and are high performance rotary shoulder connections and have higher torsional strength in comparison to API connections. The range includes all the sizes from 2 3/8" OD to 6 5/8" OD. The connections provide improved hydraulic efficiency, more flexibility on OD and ID selection and are engineered to provide additional elastic deformation during make-up.
