Hulled Sesame Seeds

  • Sesame (Sesame indicum) is one of the oldest cultivated plants in the world, prized as an oilseed for at least 5,000 years. While it is beginning to regain favor due to its exceptionally high calcium and magnesium content, few realize it is also one of the most potent medicinal foods still commonly consumed today.

  • In fact, its history as a medicine goes back 3600 years to Egyptian times where it was listed in the scrolls of the Ebers as a favored medicine. Also, women in ancient Babylon were believed to use a mixture of honey and sesame seeds havla to prolong youth and beauty, and Roman soldiers ate the mixture for strength and energy.There are two types in sesame seeds one is Natural sesame seeds and other is Hulled sesame seeds.

  • Natural Sesame Seeds: We are leading exporter of natural sesame seeds .We offer the finest quality Seeds which are whole and nutty. The originations of these seeds are from the top quality soils. Our array of natural sesame seeds are originated from the top quality soils. Before we hull, these seeds are thoroughly cleaned and graded and thereby enhance uniform quality and size of the seeds. Natural sesame seeds are largely served in bakery products such as breads, bread sticks, cookies, candies, pasta, vegetables & curry dishes.

  • Hulled Sesame seeds: Mechanically Hulled Sesame Seeds are considered as the most nutritious seeds because of its protein and valuable Minerals. The husk hull (skin) of these seeds are removed hence are called as Hulled Sesame Seeds. Prior to hulling process, these sesame seeds are thoroughly cleaned. Hulled Sesame Seeds are relatively softer and delicious as compared to Natural Sesame Seeds.
